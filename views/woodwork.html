Electronic

  • Intergalactic Call to Arms
    I got a midi controller a while back and recently I decided to compose something with it on Ableton live. I tried to make something complex in composition, like an orchestral music but using digital synths. Most of the sounds you hear here are simple square wave sounds generated using Massive digital synth. I pictured robots fighting an intergalactic space war when composing this.
  • Raise the Voltage
    Inspired by many sci-fi soundtracks, I decided to make an electronic track that would be suited for an epic sci-fi film. The synth swell in the introduction would be great for any suspense scene.

    I used my minibrute synthesizer and korg volca keys. The drumming is played live on the V-drums.
